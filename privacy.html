Privacy Policy for WHYTE ROUTE

WHYTE ROUTE (“the App”) is operated to support route planning, GPS-based driver location, shift tracking, and customer updates. This policy explains how data is collected, used, and protected.

Information We Collect

  • Location Data: Used only to provide live driver tracking and navigation inside the app.
  • User ID & Authentication Data: Managed by Firebase Authentication.
  • Route, Stops, and Shift Data: Stored securely in Firebase Firestore.

How We Use Your Data

Data is used exclusively for app functionality, including:

  • GPS tracking for drivers and customers
  • Shift tracking and timestamping
  • Route and stop management
  • Account authentication

Third-Party Services

WHYTE ROUTE uses:

  • Firebase Authentication
  • Firebase Firestore
  • Mapbox or Apple Maps

Data Sharing

No data is sold or used for advertising. Information is only shared to provide core functionality.
